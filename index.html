PROFILE

I connect research, teaching, and public policy — turning complex technical material into things people can use: clear advice, good courses, readable writing.

My PhD in computational materials science (University of Otago, thesis defended June 2026) applied DFT and CALPHAD modelling to solid-state hydrogen storage under the MBIE–BMBF funded German–NZ Green Hydrogen Centre. Before that, I spent a decade teaching undergraduate engineering in India and seven years in scientific publishing — including editing 28 of the working-group reports behind Kerala's 14th Five Year Plan. I write features for Chemistry in New Zealand, review books for the Otago Daily Times, and broadcast in Malayalam.
